Dog

Dogs are known for their loyalty and are often referred to as 'man's best friend'.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Lifespan: 10-13 years
  • Habitat: Domesticated, versatile environments
  • Diet: Omnivores (primarily meat-based diet)

Dogs have been companions to humans for thousands of years, known for their wide range of breeds, sizes, and abilities. They possess remarkable senses of smell and hearing, and are used in various roles, from assistance animals to beloved household pets.
